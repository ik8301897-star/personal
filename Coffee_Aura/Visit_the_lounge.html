// The House Rules

The Aura Code

To maintain an atmosphere of pure comfort, deep focus, and uncompromised coffee worship, we kindly ask our guests to align with our lounge culture.

Code 01

Acoustic Decorum

We strictly say no to loud, jarring commercial music. Our lounge is wired for sound absorption, playing only soft jazz, minimal vinyl tracks, or low-fi atmospheric ambient beats. Your phone calls are best kept on vibrate, and your conversations, gentle.

Code 02

The Sensory Roasting Hour

Lounge mein baithne walon ko live roasting ka real aroma milega. When our micro-roaster is running, the space fills up with pure, complex essential oils of roasting coffee. We expect our guests to cherish this sensory experience without heavy synthetic perfumes disrupting the zone.

Code 03

The Open Barista Dialogue

Our baristas are not just servers; they are certified coffee craftsmen. You are always welcome to step up to the counter, ask about the current batch origin, discuss sensory tasting notes, or customize your extraction metrics. Dialogue is celebrated here.
