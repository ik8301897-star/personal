Our Roots

From Farms to Patna

"The journey of a thousand miles, captured in a single cup."

Handpicked in South India

Our journey starts in the misty hills of Chikmagalur and Coorg. We work directly with local farmers to select 100% premium Arabica beans, ensuring perfect size, moisture, and pure quality before they leave the estates.

02

Direct to Our Patna Roastery

Without any middleman, these green beans travel straight to our hometown. Purity lock karke hum pure batch ko safe le kar aate hain, ready to handle the changing seasons of Bihar.

03

Crafted with Local Love

In Patna, we roast them in small batches every single week. This bridges the gap between premium South Indian heritage farms and your daily coffee cup, giving you the freshest aroma possible.

Mithila Craft

Mithila Makhana Frappe

Who thought popped foxnuts could redefine premium iced frappes? We slow-roast authentic Mithila Makhana, caramelize them with house blend syrups, and blend them directly into our cold craft coffee for an incredible local crunch.

Organic Local Gud Twist

Replacing commercial refined sugar with pure, unrefined local organic Jaggery (Gud). It gives our hot lattes a warm, deep, earthy molasses sweetness that reminds you of cozy winter mornings in Bihar.
