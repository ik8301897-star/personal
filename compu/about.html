WELCOME TO

VCC

Vikram Computer Centre is a Computer Education Institute (Registered Under Indian Society Reg. Act 21, 1860, Regd. No. 682/2012-13) SAI UTTHAN SEVA SANSTHAN conducting computer -oriented courses. This institute was established to cater to the basic needs in computer education. It offers courses of six months,one year,one and a half years and short term duration courses along with this various other courses are also offered through our institutes. These courses mainly benefits school and college students. Office going Executives, Housewife educated unemployed youth and those interested in taking up computer as their career.

Mission & Vision

To encourage young & competent people to opt for social entrepreneurship in educational & Training sector and to bring the skill gap in India by offering skill based training at affordable cost. To create & nature a learning and knowledge based environment, conductive to pursuit of Quality education Which would transform a socially responsible generation to act on their professional values & beliefs. To bring about their overall personality development, fostering a caring & creative environment that emphasizes, Physical, social & intellectual development.
