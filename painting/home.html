Namaste!

MITHILA PAINTS is run by a national award winning artist Bhavna Jha from Darbhanga, Bihar.

We paint an ancient artform from hearts of mithila on clothing and home decor.

We are here to narrate a story and present authentic Madhubani Painting. We are a team of 150 Rural artisans.

When you buy our products you support these artisans related to us for more than 25 years.

Love, Bhavna Jha
